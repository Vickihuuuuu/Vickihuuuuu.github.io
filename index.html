The Training Curriculum

From Recruits to Revenue Drivers: 'Why' - ‘How’ - ‘What’

Ambassadors learned the 'Why'. They understood our value proposition, competitive advantages, and the student career landscape.

2. Funnel & Process Expertise

They learned the 'How'. Training covered our entire acquisition funnel, from generating awareness (ads) to engagement (briefings).

3. Execution & Communication Skills

They mastered the 'What'. This focused on practical skills like public speaking, consultative sales, and using our SOPs.

Building the Value Ecosystem

My role was to design and negotiate a "Win-Win-Win" proposition that aligned the distinct goals of corporate partners, academic institutions, and students into one sustainable growth engine.

  • Framed the program as a high-impact CSR & branding opportunity.
  • Highlighted the value of direct Gen Z consumer insights.

🏛️ How I Engaged Academic Partners

  • Collaborated with career services to launch a module for team-based, practical experience.
  • Designed win-win revenue-sharing and co-branding models.
  • Positioned the program as the essential bridge from theory to practice.
