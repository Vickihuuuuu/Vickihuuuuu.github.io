Metric Explanations

  • Profitability: Measures the financial return of the program. High score = high margin.
  • Brand Impact: How much does this program enhance company brand's prestige and visibility? High score = significant brand lift.
  • Participant Value: The educational, career, and experiential value delivered to the students. High score = transformative experience.
  • Operational Efficiency: Preparation Time, this positive metric measures how easy the program is to run, scale, and replicate. A program with long prep time has low efficiency. High score = streamlined and scalable.
  • Partner Integration: How deeply and strategically does this program engage our corporate and academic partners? High score = deep, mutually beneficial partnerships.
  • Innovation Level: Does this program represent a unique, cutting-edge offering, or is it a more traditional model? High score = highly innovative.
